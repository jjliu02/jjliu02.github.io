I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at Beihang University (BUAA), China. I obtained my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Technology from Beihang University in 2024.

My research focuses on large language model (LLM) agents, with particular interests in autonomous agent systems, agent reasoning and planning, multi-agent collaboration, and the evaluation and benchmarking of intelligent agents.
